Frequently Asked Questions

Balayage is a French hair coloring technique where color is hand-painted onto the hair to create natural, sun-kissed highlights with soft, seamless transitions. Unlike traditional highlights, balayage creates a more natural, lived-in look.
Ombre balayage combines both techniques - ombre creates a gradual transition from dark to light, while balayage adds hand-painted highlights for dimension. The result is a more natural, blended look than traditional ombre.
Balayage typically lasts 3-4 months and grows out beautifully with minimal maintenance. Because the color is hand-painted and blended naturally, there are no harsh lines as it grows out.
